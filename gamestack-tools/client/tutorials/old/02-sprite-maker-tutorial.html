Creating a Gamestack: SpriteMaker Sprite()

Step 1: Open the SpriteMaker Tool

  1. Select New >> Sprite

Step 2: Create a new Animation in the Sprite.animations array[]

  1. Assign image and frameSize properties
  2. Assign the frameBounds.min,frameBounds.max, and frameBounds.termPoint
  3. View the Animation to verify it is correct

Step 3: Apply a sound to the animation.

  1. Create the sound
  2. Right click your animation from Step 2 and click 'bind-events'
  3. Bind the sound to the object

Step 4: Create a jump motion.

  1. * The jump only needs to bring the character 'up' (gravity will handle the descent, once added to any implementation of your sprite.
  2. Test the jump motion, by clicking it once, then clicking play

Step 6: Create the 'magic spell' projectile.

  1. STEPS FOR MAGIC SPELL
  2. Add a sound to the Projectile
  3. COMPLETE STEP 6
Now our sprite has one animation, one 'jump' movement, and one projectile.
